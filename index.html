Bhaiji Group of Companies: A Legacy of Excellence

Bhaiji Infra Projects Pvt. Ltd. is a 25-year-old legacy company founded by the visionary Mr. Shyam Borele, whose dedication and hard work laid the foundation for the company's remarkable journey. Supported by Ms. Sushma Borele, a pillar of strength, and Ms. Samiksha Borele, a dynamic leader driving innovation, the company exemplifies a harmonious blend of experience and forward-thinking leadership. Over the past two and a half decades, Borele world has evolved into a diversified enterprise with operations spanning real estate, financial services, hospitality, commercial development, and residential development.
