BORELE WORLD

Bhaiji Group: A Legacy of Excellence

Bhaiji Infra Projects Pvt. Ltd. is a 25-year-old legacy company founded by the visionary Mr. Shyam Borele, whose dedication and hard work laid the foundation for the company's remarkable journey. Supported by Ms. Sushma Borele, a pillar of strength, and Ms. Samiksha Borele, a dynamic leader driving innovation, the company exemplifies a harmonious blend of experience and forward-thinking leadership. Over the past two and a half decades, Borele world has evolved into a diversified enterprise with operations spanning real estate, financial services, hospitality, commercial development, and residential development.

Meet Our Directors

Mr. Shyam Borele

Chairman

Mr. Shyam Borele

Chairman

A visionary entrepreneur and a self-made business leader, Mr. Shyam Borele is a name synonymous with determination, strategic foresight, and excellence in business. His journey is nothing short of inspiring—starting at the young age of 18, he stepped into the world of business with a deep passion and an unshakable work ethic. His initial foray was in the textile industry, where he honed his business acumen before diversifying into electronics. With an instinct for identifying lucrative opportunities, he ventured into automobile finance, laying the foundation for what would soon become one of the most remarkable success stories in the Indian automobile sector. His entry into the automobile industry as an authorized Maruti dealer was a game-changer. For over 15 years, he not only dominated the market but also set new benchmarks for dealership excellence. His showroom was recognized as the Best Dealership of its time, earning prestigious awards for the Best Showroom and the Best Workshop of the Year—a testament to his commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and operational excellence. After conquering the automobile sector, Mr. Borele expanded his vision into real estate. His strategic investments and high-value deals in Nagpur’s real estate market placed him at the forefront of the industry. His ability to forge strong alliances with major corporations has further solidified his reputation as a business powerhouse. Today, he continues to lead and innovate, successfully managing NBFC operations, providing financial solutions that drive growth and development. His journey—from textiles to electronics, automobiles, real estate, and finance—exemplifies the spirit of entrepreneurship and adaptability. A true leader, Mr. Shyam Borele stands as an inspiration for aspiring entrepreneurs, proving that vision, hard work, and the courage to take risks can transform dreams into reality.

Fashion and Textile Industry: Ramdhani Agencies Pvt. Ltd.

Ramdhani Agencies Private Limited (RAPL) is a reputable Indian private limited company with a rich history spanning over two decades. Incorporated on October 25, 2004, the company has established itself as a prominent player in the fashion and textile industry. Headquartered in Nagpur, Maharashtra, India, RAPL operates with legacy of quality. The company is led by a team of two directors, Mr. Shyam Borele and Mrs. Sushma Borele, who have been instrumental in driving the company's growth and success. RAPL 's commitment to transparency and compliance with regulatory requirements has earned it a reputation as a trustworthy and reliable business partner. Throughout its 20-year journey, RAPL has demonstrated its ability to adapt to changing market trends and customer needs. As a private limited company, RAPL continues to evolve and grow, driven by its passion for innovation and excellence in the fashion and textile industry.
