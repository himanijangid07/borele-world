Bhaiji Group: A Legacy of Excellence

Bhaiji Infra Projects Pvt. Ltd. is a 25-year-old legacy company founded by the visionary Mr. Shyam Borele, whose dedication and hard work laid the foundation for the company's remarkable journey. Supported by Ms. Sushma Borele, a pillar of strength, and Ms. Samiksha Borele, a dynamic leader driving innovation, the company exemplifies a harmonious blend of experience and forward-thinking leadership. Over the past two and a half decades, Borele world has evolved into a diversified enterprise with operations spanning real estate, financial services, hospitality, commercial development, and residential development.

Fashion and Textile Industry: Ramdhani Agencies Pvt. Ltd.

Ramdhani Agencies Private Limited (RAPL) is a reputable Indian private limited company with a rich history spanning over two decades. Incorporated on October 25, 2004, the company has established itself as a prominent player in the fashion and textile industry. Headquartered in Nagpur, Maharashtra, India, RAPL operates with legacy of quality. The company is led by a team of two directors, Mr. Shyam Borele and Mrs. Sushma Borele, who have been instrumental in driving the company's growth and success. RAPL 's commitment to transparency and compliance with regulatory requirements has earned it a reputation as a trustworthy and reliable business partner. Throughout its 20-year journey, RAPL has demonstrated its ability to adapt to changing market trends and customer needs. As a private limited company, RAPL continues to evolve and grow, driven by its passion for innovation and excellence in the fashion and textile industry.
