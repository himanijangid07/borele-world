Shyam Borele

Chairman

A visionary entrepreneur and a self-made business leader, Mr. Shyam Borele is a name synonymous with determination, strategic foresight, and excellence in business. His journey is nothing short of inspiring—starting at the young age of 18, he stepped into the world of business with a deep passion and an unshakable work ethic.

His initial foray was in the textile industry, where he honed his business acumen before diversifying into electronics. With an instinct for identifying lucrative opportunities, he ventured into automobile finance, laying the foundation for what would soon become one of the most remarkable success stories in the Indian automobile sector.

His entry into the automobile industry as an authorized Maruti dealer was a game-changer. For over 15 years, he not only dominated the market but also set new benchmarks for dealership excellence. His showroom was recognized as the Best Dealership of its time, earning prestigious awards for the Best Showroom and the Best Workshop of the Year—a testament to his commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and operational excellence.

After conquering the automobile sector, Mr. Borele expanded his vision into real estate. His strategic investments and high-value deals in Nagpur’s real estate market placed him at the forefront of the industry. His ability to forge strong alliances with major corporations has further solidified his reputation as a business powerhouse. Today, he continues to lead and innovate, successfully managing NBFC operations, providing financial solutions that drive growth and development. His journey—from textiles to electronics, automobiles, real estate, and finance—exemplifies the spirit of entrepreneurship and adaptability. A true leader, Mr. Shyam Borele stands as an inspiration for aspiring entrepreneurs, proving that vision, hard work, and the courage to take risks can transform dreams into reality.

For more than 30 years, she has stood shoulder to shoulder with Mr. Shyam Borele and the leadership team, shaping the company’s journey with her unwavering commitment. From its early days to its present stature, her presence has been instrumental in every milestone, every triumph. Her dedication is not just about managing finances—it is about nurturing a legacy, inspiring growth, and leading with quiet yet profound strength. Mrs. Sushma Borele is not just the strength behind Bhaiji infraprojects Pvt Ltd she is its heartbeat, its guiding light, and the unwavering force that ensures its continued success.

Dr. Samiksha Borele

President

Dr. Samiksha Borele is a seasoned entrepreneur, transformational life coach, and positive mindset mentor, currently leading as the President of Borele World—a premier enterprise renowned for its excellence in real estate, hospitality, and strategic innovation. With a robust track record in real estate development, hospitality management, marketing strategy, human resources, and international business, she has been instrumental in driving sustained business growth and operational excellence. As a multi-faceted leader, Dr. Borele also serves as the Trustee of the Borele Foundation and holds directorships at Bhaiji Infraprojects Pvt. Ltd., SB Hospitality Pvt. Ltd., and Ruchika Infra Pvt. Ltd. Her entrepreneurial vision is complemented by a deep commitment to innovation, quality, and customer-centricity across every venture she leads.

Her global outlook is shaped by her participation in esteemed international programs, including the Global Management Challenge at La Trobe University, Australia, and executive education at Stansfield College, Singapore, and the Global Opportunities & Threat Analysis Program (GOTA) by IIPM. These experiences enriched her strategic thinking, cross-cultural business acumen, and leadership agility.

Dr. Borele is also a renowned life coach and wellness mentor, passionately advocating the power of a positive mindset in achieving success and fulfillment. Her journey reflects an unwavering dedication to excellence, personal transformation, and the creation of impactful business ecosystems that are both visionary and value-driven.
